# Forge Project — Naming Research

**Date:** 2026-03-18
**Purpose:** Brand/namespace conflict check for 20 candidate names for the "Forge" AI development orchestrator rename, plus 15 additional brainstormed candidates.

---

## Scoring Rubric

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Uniqueness** | 5 = no conflicts found anywhere; 1 = major existing project with that exact name |
| **CLI Feel** | 5 = crisp, short, natural as a command (`name build`); 1 = awkward to type/say |
| **Evocativeness** | 5 = strongly evokes engineering authority/orchestration/judgment; 1 = no relevant associations |

## Decision Guidance

**If you want maximum authority/Latin gravitas:** Duxar or Duxon (dux = the leader in Latin and academic tradition)

**If you want maximum CLI crispness + unique sound:** Arxon (`arxon build` — two punchy syllables, hard consonants)

**If you want to name the orchestration function directly:** Ordax (order + action; immediately clear what it does)

**If you want Roman/praetorian leadership feel:** Praex (praetor-derived; "the one who goes first and leads")

**If you want a softer, more synthesis-oriented feel:** Synthar (coordinates many things into one coherent whole)

**Names to avoid:** Arken, Kovan, Ordis, Nexar, Lumis, Primex, Syndex, Orin, Helmix, Kairos — all have significant existing software conflicts.
